A nice little coffee spot not too far from Oimachi station. There's a small seating area on the 1st floor, and some more seating upstairs as well. I like the seats on the 2nd floor facing the windows. Power is available, I think WiFi might be available, but I'm not really sure. Staff was very friendly and welcoming :)
